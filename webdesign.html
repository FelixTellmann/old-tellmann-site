Getting started, Together

You have been thinking about this new website and perhaps even gathered some resources. Now it’s time to get your idea out into the world. Let’s do things right from the very beginning and give you a head start on competition.

With the aim to build long term relationships, all projects are started with a conversation. This is where we sit down, grab a cup of coffee and dial in the details. Understanding the task at hand and ironing out the wrinkles is key.

Firstly, we need to get under the skin of your business to discover who you really are, research your marketplace, and determine your objectives.

Once we've aligned the details, it's time to get things mapped out and organized in a solid plan. This part is really crucial in keeping the project in line to completion while ensuring accountability.

Building Success

Once the planning is done it's time to bring those ideas and plans to life. I strongly believe in clear and open hearted communication for the best results, making the development process collaborative and transparent.

I create your website in step-by-step development process, from information architecture, prototypes & design to the actual coding. Whats important, is that I keep you consistently in the loop to provide opportunities for review and feedback.

Once you are happy with the look and feel of the website, I will complete the last tweaks, test the page thoroughly and get your new website ready for launch.

Care
Beyond Launch

Websites are living, breathing entities and need constant care and maintenance.

Updating content, making changes to the back-end and fixing broken links are all in a day’s work. All of these phases are critical to the Web design process.

But the thread that runs through the process is strategy: the desire to achieve a goal, to move the organization forward, to prosper in a competitive environment.

After the site launches, I offer support and training as well as being on-hand should you require any further service.
