​ Welcome, my name is Felix Tellmann, founder of SmartUp Online and freelancing Webdesigner.

​ I am proud German, living in Cape Town. I'm on a mission to connect people over good food, technology and intuitive design. I combine my experience in restaurant management, my education from UCT and my talent in web-development to help restaurants build successful brands.

I’ve spent most of my life deeply interested in technology and food continuously building things with both. As a teenager, I was a classic computer nerd, spending most of my times messing with the computer, doing 1 of 4 things: coding with software and programs and figuring things out. Tinkering with the hardware and building/ruining computers by experimenting too much. Building websites with FrontPage 98 and Flash. And of course, playing games.

Most of that is still true today.

I love building things, challenging ideas, seeking knowledge and creating opportunities. I enjoy finding patterns in things, constructing models for how the world works, and then discussing, sharing, and using that information to improve the world in some way.

My professional expertise lies in the Restaurant industry with specialized focus on new business development, strategy, training & development, branding & webdesign, POS programming, menu development, kitchen management and cost control.
